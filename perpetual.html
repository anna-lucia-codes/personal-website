Perpetual OscillationsÂ extends my exploration of ambient visuals first developed inArt For Walls In Public Spaces. Using the same generative techniques: layered, looping lines in shifting phases, I sought to create a series of works that are constantly in motion, yet quietly meditative.

Released as a collection of 100 NFTs on fx(hash), each iteration offers a unique unfolding rhythm, inviting viewers to experience a visual equivalent of ambient music: endlessly variable, subtly immersive, and open to both fleeting attention and deep contemplation.
